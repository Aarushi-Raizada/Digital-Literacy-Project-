## Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic

In this task, I created a one-page infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. I included topics like safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, and the importance of having a professional online presence.

While making the infographic, I found it a bit challenging to decide what content to include and how to keep it simple at the same time. This task helped me understand how to present information in a clear and visually appealing way.
