TASK-4: Part A
 ## Do’s 
   * 	Think before you post — make sure your content is appropriate and respectful. 
   *	Maintain a positive and professional profile, especially if it can be seen by teachers or recruiters. 
   *	Use privacy settings wisely to control who can see your posts and personal information. 
   *	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news. 
   *	Engage respectfully in comments and discussions, even if you disagree. 

## Don’ts
   *	Don’t share personal details like phone number, address, or bank information publicly. 
   *	Don’t post offensive or harmful content that can hurt others or damage your reputation. 
   *	Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people without checking their profile. 
   *	Don’t spend excessive time on social media, especially during study hours. 
   *	Don’t believe or forward everything blindly without checking its authenticity.
