## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never scan unknown QR codes to receive money — QR codes are mostly used to pay, not receive.  
2. Always verify UPI requests before approving; check the name and amount carefully.  
3. Do not share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.  
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links sent via SMS, email, or social media.  
5. Use strong and unique passwords for apps and enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.  
6. Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores (Play Store/App Store).  
7. Be cautious while buying or selling online — don’t trust people who create urgency or offer deals that seem too good to be true.  
8. Regularly check bank statements and transaction history to spot any unusual activity early.  
9. Log out from shared devices and avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.  
10. Update your phone and apps regularly to stay protected from security vulnerabilities.
